About Us

Online Counselling Booking Platform is a web-based solution built to promote mental well-being and personal growth. It enables users to receive article recommendations based on their interests and also allows them to book sessions with registered counsellors easily.

The platform ensures a smooth user experience with secure login, role-based access for clients and counsellors, and an appointment booking system.

  • Personalized article suggestions
  • Role-based login and registration
  • Online counselling appointment booking
  • Counsellor management via admin panel

How It Works

  1. Register as client/counsellor
  2. Explore articles & counsellors
  3. Book a session & grow
